# SolarScorch

## Solar Geometry, Spectral Science, CA673 Solar Modelling

**Author:** Richard Steven Vallance (Da Valenca)

## Purpose

Solar geometry and spectral science research arising from Codex Atlanticus f.673r (CA673) and the Turin Shroud solar scorch hypothesis. Contains solar azimuth calculations, shadow bearing tests, refraction modelling, and observational geometry.

---

## Key Finding

CA673 encodes a predominantly solar shadow programme (six of seven target bearings confirmed to sub-1.5° error). One anomalous bearing (156.4°) cannot be resolved by solar geometry alone — evidence presently suggests a non-solar or reflected-light source.

---

## Evidential Limitations

Solar modelling results require independent astronomical verification. One bearing remains unresolved within the solar hypothesis.
